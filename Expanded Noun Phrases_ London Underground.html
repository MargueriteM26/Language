Learn It ☁️

An expanded noun phrase adds detail to a noun. Use a determiner (a, the, an), one or more adjectives, and sometimes a prepositional phrase. Look at these London Underground examples:

The grey cloud
A heavy cold shower over the Thames
The thick grey fog across Tower Bridge

Let's look at more examples on the Underground:

An ancient dark tunnel beneath the city
The overcrowded noisy platform at rush hour
A bewildered tourist with a heavy suitcase
